### Title:

User Story: Displaying Chat History Grouped by Date with Per-Item Downloads

### Description:

As a user, I want to view my chat history grouped by date with the option to download individual conversations, so that I can easily review and manage my conversation records.

### Acceptance Criteria:

- The application displays the chat history in chronological order, grouped by date.
- Each group is labeled with the corresponding date range.
- Users have the ability to click on a specific conversation to view its details.
- Users can download individual conversations as a PDF or CSV file.
- The downloaded files are accurate and include all relevant information from the original conversation.
